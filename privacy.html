Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-05. Written in plain English.

The short version

KiddieDaily doesn't collect personal information from you or your child by default. There are no accounts required for the Kid Zone. Game progress and reading history live in your own browser's local storage. The only third-party code on the site is Google AdSense, when ads are enabled. The section below covers what AdSense does and doesn't see.

Information we collect directly

For the Kid Zone (default view): none. We don't ask for names, emails, ages, parent contact info, location, or any other identifying detail. We don't run Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Hotjar, FullStory, or similar trackers.

For the Parent Zone (when you choose to unlock it): you may optionally provide an email address and a PIN, plus topic preferences. This information is stored encrypted in our database (Supabase) and is used only to deliver the personalized features you've signed up for. We never sell it, share it, or use it for advertising.

What our server sees

Standard HTTP server logs: your IP address, your browser type, and the URL you requested. We use that data only for the operational basics: serving pages, blocking abuse, and keeping the site up. Logs are retained no longer than 30 days.

What your browser stores locally

Your browser saves your current page, reading position, theme preferences (if any), and whether you've unlocked Parent Zone on this device. This data stays on your device. We can't see it. Clearing your browser data clears it.

Google AdSense (when ads are running)

When ads are enabled on the site, they're served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve those ads. We don't share any of our data with Google; the relationship is between you (your browser) and Google directly.

For Kid Zone pageviews, we flag traffic as child-directed in our AdSense settings. This requires Google to serve only contextual, non-personalized ads on those pages.

For Parent Zone pageviews (once Parent Zone launches), standard AdSense personalization may apply, subject to your consent and your AdSense personalization settings (manageable at adssettings.google.com).

Google's full privacy policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children under 13 (COPPA)

The Kid Zone is designed for general audiences including families with young children. Because we don't collect personal information from anyone in the Kid Zone, we don't fall under COPPA's parental-consent requirements for collecting children's personal information.

The Parent Zone is intended for adults. If you believe we've inadvertently received personal information from a child, please email us and we'll delete it.

EU / UK / California residents

If you're in a jurisdiction with strong privacy laws (GDPR, GDPR-K, CCPA), you have rights to access, correct, and delete personal information held about you. For Kid Zone use we hold no personal information about you, so there's nothing to access or delete. For Parent Zone, you can email us with a verified request and we'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies + similar technologies

Kid Zone: local storage only (preferences). No cookies set by us.

Parent Zone (when launched): essential cookies for the PIN session and preference cookies for your settings. No third-party tracking cookies set by us.

AdSense, when ads are running, may set its own cookies as described in Google's policy above.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date and post a notice on the homepage for at least 30 days.
