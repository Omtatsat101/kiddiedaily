How we choose stories

We read coverage from AP, Reuters, NPR, BBC, PBS Kids, and a small set of trusted science and parenting sources. We summarize stories that are interesting, true, and safe for a 7-12 year old to read. We skip graphic violence, sexual content, and anything that frames addiction, self-harm, or dangerous stunts approvingly.

Every story links to its sources. If a story has a contested claim, we route it to our fact check section and explain the evidence.
