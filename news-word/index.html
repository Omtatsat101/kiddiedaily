🧠 News Word

Today’s secret word is a real science or nature term from today's science headlines. Guess it in six tries — green means right spot, yellow means right letter/wrong spot. Solve it to unlock the story behind the word!

Today’s news More games
How to play
Type a 6-letter word and press Enter. After each guess the tiles change color:
Green = right letter, right spot. Yellow = right letter, wrong spot. Gray = that letter isn’t in the word.
You get six guesses. The answer is always a word from a real, kid-friendly science or nature story — no scary or grown-up news here.
🧠 For grown-ups

News Word is a Wordle-style vocabulary game where the daily answer is a genuine science or nature word pulled from that day’s kid-safe headlines. It builds spelling, pattern-thinking, and curiosity — and every solved word links to the real story so learning keeps going.

It runs entirely in the browser: no accounts, no cookies, nothing your child types is ever uploaded. Looking for more? Try our Games and Draw the News pages.
