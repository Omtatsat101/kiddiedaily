How to use KiddieDaily with your family
1

Read the bias bar. Every article shows which outlets covered it and whether they lean left, center, or right. No single outlet is always wrong — or always right.

2

Use the discussion guide. Each article has 3 parent-specific prompts — what to ask your child, how to check facts, and how to explore further.

Understanding bias ratings

KiddieDaily uses AllSides and Ad Fontes Media bias ratings — the same methodology used by researchers and journalism schools.

Scale: -2 = far left  |  -1 = leans left  |  0 = center  |  +1 = leans right  |  +2 = far right

No media source is perfectly neutral. Our goal is to show you the landscape — so you can read with your eyes open and form your own family's view.
