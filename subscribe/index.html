Add to Home Screen Free · iPhone & Android

Turn KiddieDaily into a home screen app — no app store needed. Open the site in Safari or Chrome, then add it to your home screen for one-tap daily access.

▶ iPhone/Safari instructions
  1. Open kiddiedaily.com in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow) at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Name it "KiddieDaily" and tap Add
▶ Android/Chrome instructions
  1. Open kiddiedaily.com in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap Add
