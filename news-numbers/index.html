🔢 News by the Numbers

Every big story has a number in it. Guess how big it is first — then find out how much that number really means. This is how you build number sense!

🎯 Warm-up number
100,000,000

That's like counting every second for over 30 years without stopping!

How to play

  1. Read the headline — the number is hidden with question marks.
  2. Type your best guess. Nobody expects it to be exact!
  3. Hit Reveal to see the real number and how close you were.
  4. Read the “how big is it really?” clue to feel the size of the number.

📊 For grown-ups

“News by the Numbers” turns real headlines into estimation practice — the same skill grown-ups use to sanity-check prices, distances, and data. Estimating first, then checking, is one of the strongest ways to build lasting number sense.
